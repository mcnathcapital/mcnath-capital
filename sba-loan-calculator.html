What SBA loan programs are available in 2026?

SBA 7(a) Standard: Up to $5M for any business purpose
SBA Express: Up to $500K with 36-48 hour approval
SBA 504: Up to $5.5M for real estate and equipment
SBA Microloans: Up to $50K for startups and small businesses

How long does SBA loan approval take in 2026?

Express loans: 36-48 hours for qualified borrowers
Standard 7(a) loans: 30-45 days with preferred lenders
504 loans: 45-60 days (real estate transactions)
We work exclusively with SBA preferred lenders to minimize processing times.

What are current SBA loan rates?

As of February 2026, SBA loan rates range from Prime + 2.75% to Prime + 6.50% depending on loan size and program. With prime rate around 8.5%, most borrowers qualify for rates between 11.25% - 14.50%. Express loans carry slightly higher rates but offer faster approval.

Can startups qualify for SBA loans?

Yes! While most SBA programs require 2+ years in business, several options exist for newer businesses: SBA Microloans (up to $50K), SBA Express for startups with strong credit (up to $500K), and franchise financing programs. Personal credit score and industry experience are key factors.
